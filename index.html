Mr. Joel A. Hawod, UNIFFIED’s 2021 Most Influential Teacher Awardee

The United Federation of Fil-Am Educators, Inc. and Xavier School are pleased to announce the selection of MR. JOEL A. HAWOD, of the SHS IB Department, as one of UNIFFIED’s 2021 Most Influential Teacher awardees. This award honors educators who promoted excellence and demonstrated a significant impact on others. They served as role models in many aspects inside and outside the classroom.

ABOUT US

Founded and managed by the Society of Jesus, the school is a K-12 school, offering a three-level curriculum: Early Education, Grade School, and High School. Xavier School is also an IB World School, authorized to offer the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program (IBDP), a rigorous and innovative academic program recognized by universities worldwide.
